Painting Series

Between Two Springs

Spring in Barcelona arrives gently. Warm air, the scent of flowers, light that makes everything feel soft and pleasant. It is easy to surrender to it.

But spring also arrives in Lebanon, where I have roots and people I love. There, the same season carries a different weight: dust and rubble alongside blossoms, beauty that exists in spite of everything.

Between Two Springs is born from that contradiction and from the guilt of living in sweetness while those close to me live in the shadow of war. The paintings hold both truths at once: flowers push through, colour flickers with hope, but the charcoal and Chinese ink stain everything, seep into the edges, refuse to let the light be simple.

These works do not resolve the tension. They sit inside it.
